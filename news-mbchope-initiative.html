For a brighter future, MBChope initiative

Posted On: March 21, 2023

In collaboration with ACE, MBC Group launched a new heartwarming initiative aiming at aiding young high schoolers acquire skills that would equip them to face the challenges of the future. Tuesday, 21st March was the start of ACE's beehive activity supported by the most professional media companies in the Middle East.

MBC's Professional Preparation Programme (برنامج التهيئة المهنيّة) will extend over six months where around 100 young high-school males and females aged 17-20 will follow an upskilling program instructed by ACE trainers.

The Professional Preparation program aims at:

  • equipping these highschoolers to enter university,

  • giving them the necessary skills to establish their business,

  • giving them a head start in finding a job.

The areas covered in the program are:

  • Limproving their English skills,

  • improving their behavioral and human skills,

  • improving their technical and digital skills.
