American Consulting Experts

who we are

Established in 2003 by Dr. Ali Soueidan –lecturer for more than 10 years at AUB and LAU, Lebanon – American Consulting Experts (ACE) is one of the leading training and consultancy companies in the Middle East. After establishing its headquarters in Huston-Texas, ACE has expanded its offices to Saudi Arabia, UAE, Qatar and Lebanon.

With its 50 professional instructors, ACE takes pride in being the leader in learning management system (LMS) industry and corporate training. ACE has also partnered with six leading associations: Project Management Institute (PMI), Logical Operations, ISM, CERTNEXUS, The CPD Certification Service (CPD CERTIFIED), and The Institute of Leadership & Management in order to provide its customers with the most reliable training courses and certificates. ACE thus strives to create value and ensure its customers’ short term and strategic targets by providing modern and customized technical and soft skills; sales training workshops and E-learning XAPI courses; LMS and consultancy services that support corporate business companies and financial institutions.

Mission Statement

ACE brings cutting edge solutions to clients by providing the latest advanced solutions through qualified and customized programs to meet customers’ business and strategic short term and long-term needs.

ACE team adopts a set of unquestioned values: integrity & respect, professional excellence, customer orientation, creativity, passion, and team spirit.

Our Vision & Goal

Fueled by the sole purpose of helping our clients thrive in a fast-moving technological era, we at ACE aim at supporting the development of our clients’ companies’ business models, redefining their business practices, enhancing their business knowledge and implementing the best practices to achieve their strategic goals and visions.

We work closely with our clients to improve professional business communication channels in the workplace and among the different operational, support, control and supervisory levels. Our state-of-the-art learning approach coupled with our implementation of forefront consulting and training methods aims at advancing all stakeholders: empowering our clients to grow and deliver integrated solutions.
