Established in 2009 by Dr. Allan Soueidan –lecturer for more than 10 years at AUB (American University of Beirut) and LAU (Lebanese American University), – American Consulting Experts (ACE) takes pride at becoming the leading training and consulting companies in the Middle East. This story of success began after establishing ACE headquarters in Houston -Texas, to then expanding to KSA, UAE, and Oman.

With its 70+ professional instructors, ACE is the transformation partner you can trust. Having forged strong partnerships with six leading associations: Project Management Institute (PMI), Logical Operations (LO), Institute of Sales Management (ISM), CERTNEXUS, The CPD Certification Service (CPD), and The Institute of Leadership & Management (ILM), ACE provides its customers with the most reliable credentials and certifications. ACE thus strives to help its client achieve the most innovative and comprehensive workforce transformation solutions for their business, create value and ensure its customers’ short term and strategic targets.

Mission Statement

ACE brings cutting edge solutions to clients by providing the latest advanced solutions through qualified and customized programs to meet customers’ business and strategic short term and long-term needs.

ACE team adopts a set of unquestioned values: integrity & respect, professional excellence, customer orientation, creativity, passion, and team spirit.

Our Vision & Goal

Fueled by the sole purpose of helping our clients thrive in a fast-moving technological era, we at ACE aim at supporting the development of our clients’ companies’ business models, redefining their business practices, enhancing their business knowledge and implementing the best practices to achieve their strategic goals and visions.

We work closely with our clients to improve professional business communication channels in the workplace and among the different operational, support, control and supervisory levels. Our state-of-the-art learning approach coupled with our implementation of forefront consulting and training methods aims at advancing all stakeholders: empowering our clients to grow and deliver integrated solutions.
